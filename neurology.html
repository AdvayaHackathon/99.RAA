Neurology

The Branch of Medicine Focused on the Nervous System

What is Neurology?

Neurology is the specialized field of medicine that deals with disorders of the nervous system. This includes the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves, and muscles.

Neurologists diagnose and treat a wide range of conditions, from common issues like migraines and epilepsy to complex diseases such as multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, and stroke.

Key Areas

  • Brain Anatomy & Function
  • Spinal Cord Injuries
  • Neurodegenerative Diseases
  • Stroke and Cerebrovascular Diseases
  • Epilepsy and Seizure Disorders
  • Headaches and Migraines
